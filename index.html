How the outreach system works

This is the cold email system Alexana set up and ran for Skyglow Drones. It emails potential drone-show buyers a short sequence, and anyone interested books a call through a landing page.

Prepared by Alexana

Overview

How it works

The system runs in five steps, from building a contact list to a booked call.

  1. Find the buyers

    Search a contact database for the exact job titles and places we want.

  2. Clean the list

    Remove bad, duplicate, or wrong-fit contacts.

  3. Verify the emails

    Check every address is real before the outreach begins.

  4. Send the outreach

    A short 5-email sequence goes out over about two weeks.

  5. Book the call

    Anyone interested lands on a page and books a call with Michael.

The moving parts

The tools and subscriptions

Each tool has one clear role. Tap a card to see what it does, who pays, and its current status.

ApolloFind business contacts $99/moMichael’s

A searchable database of business contacts. We filter by job title, industry, and location, then export the matching email addresses. Its job is done once the prospect list is built.

Cost: $99/month · Paid by Michael · Status: Can pause

InstantlySend and track outreach emails $97/moMichael’s

This sends the 5-email sequence to each prospect, tests two subject lines to see which gets opened more, tracks opens and clicks, and gathers every reply in one inbox. It also keeps the sending accounts trusted by email providers.

Cost: $97/month · Paid by Michael · Status: Can pause

GoHighLevelLanding page, form, and booking $97/moMichael’s

This hosts the landing page linked from the emails. When someone fills out the form, it saves their information and automatically sends a welcome email with a booking link. It also keeps a simple customer list and tracks each deal from first contact to booked show.

Cost: $97/month · Paid by Michael · Status: Can pause after the landing page moves to skyglowdrones.com

Microsoft 365Keep everyday business email separate ExistingMichael’s

This runs Michael’s everyday business email at skyglowdrones.com. It is completely separate from cold outreach.

Cost: Existing business expense · Paid by Michael · Status: Keep

ZapmailManage dedicated outreach inboxes $25/moAlexana’s setup

This created 10 sending inboxes in bulk and configured them so email providers treat the messages as legitimate. These inboxes stay separate from Michael’s real business email.

Cost: $25/month · Alexana infrastructure · Status: Winds down while outreach is paused

Sending domainsProtect Skyglow’s main web address ~$4/moAlexana’s setup

Five web addresses on Cloudflare were used only for cold email. Keeping them separate protects skyglowdrones.com from outreach risk.

Cost: About $4/month, or about $50/year · Alexana infrastructure · Status: Winds down while outreach is paused

This is Michael’s primary web address. It also holds the email-trust records that help his real business email reach inboxes instead of spam folders.

Paid by Michael · Status: Keep permanently
